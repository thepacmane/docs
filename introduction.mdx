## What is Pacmane

Pacmane is the first memecoin launched on Blast mainnet with 1 Billion total supply. 95% of total supply verifiably provided to community at launch.

- No VC's
- No presale
- No private round
- 30 lines of code token contract (no tax, no fees, no limits)
- Locked tokens are in verified contracts
